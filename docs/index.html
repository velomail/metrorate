Privacy Policy

MetroRate is built with a privacy-first mindset. We understand the sensitivity of sales data and earnings information.

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally, in your Chrome browser's storage. We do not have a centralized database where your deals are stored.

Secure Payments

Payment processing for the Unlimited Plan is handled through secure third-party providers. We never see your credit card details.

No Tracking

We do not track your browsing history or use cookies for analytics. Your activity inside the extension remains private to you.

Export Anytime

You own your data. You can export your entire deal history to a CSV file at any time from the settings menu.
