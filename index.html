About MetroRate

A commission tracker built for people who move fast. No clutter — just your numbers, next to your pipeline.

How it works

  • Log a deal — Type a deal name and value (e.g. ACME 12,500). MetroRate applies your commission rate and saves it.
  • Dashboard — Total commission, pipeline value, and a progress bar toward your goal. Recent deals at a glance.
  • History — Every deal you log is stored locally in your browser. Your data stays on your machine.
  • Presets — Save product templates for one-tap logging. Set your commission rate and visibility (blur amounts when you need privacy).

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

MetroRate is a Chrome extension designed to help you track commissions and deals. We take your privacy seriously. By default, MetroRate processes information locally in your browser and does not send your deal data to our servers.

2. Data stored by the extension

We store the following in Chrome storage on your device only:

  • Deal information you enter (deal names, values, calculated commission amounts).
  • Presets you configure (labels and amounts for one-tap logging).
  • Settings (commission rate, visibility/blur preference, onboarding state).
  • A count of how many deals you logged per day (to enforce the free daily limit).

This data is stored locally and is not sent to our servers as part of normal operation. We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising.

3. Payment information

Payments for MetroRate are processed by Stripe on a secure checkout page. The extension does not process or store payment card numbers or bank details. When you make a one-time purchase via our landing page, Stripe may collect and store payment card details, billing name and email, and billing address in accordance with Stripe’s privacy policy.

4. How we use your information

  • To provide access to the one-time unlimited upgrade and process payments via Stripe.
  • To handle support and communicate about service changes.
  • To improve the extension in aggregate (we do not mine your deal data).

5. Data sharing

We may share limited information with Stripe (payments) and email providers (transactional emails). We do not sell or rent your personal data.

6. Your choices

  • Uninstall the extension at any time; local data is removed with the extension.
  • Request a refund for your one-time purchase by contacting us, subject to our refund policy.
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
